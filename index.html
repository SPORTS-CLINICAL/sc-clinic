Sync & backup

Export sync bundle

Creates an encrypted snapshot of everything on this device. Save it to your SPORTS CLINICAL folder in iCloud Drive; it is both your sync hand-off and your backup.

Import sync bundle

Open the latest bundle from the other device. Records merge safely: treatment entries always combine; if the same detail was edited on both devices, the newer edit is kept.

Routine: finish on one device → Export → save to iCloud Drive. On the other device → Import the newest bundle before working.

Security

Data is encrypted on this device with your password. The app locks automatically after 10 minutes idle.

Quick unlock uses this device's biometric gate for convenience; your password remains the root key and is always accepted.

Danger zone

Erases every patient record and document from this device. Export a bundle first.
